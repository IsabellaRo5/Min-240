You Are Not Alone

A website for victims of sexual assault

The Alone Project is a websie to help victims of sexual assault to not feel alone and see that their voices are vauled.

The Goal; to provide resources and support to people in order for them to be able to move on in their daily lives after the fact. It is also there to make sure that people are able to find the necessary resources in one place

Stories; a feature available for anyone who feels like this has only happened to them, where they can read success stories of other victims.

Help; a feature that finds local support groups in your area and the proper people to contact. It also guides you through the steps of reporting a sexual assault.

I got the idea of The Alone Project from talking to some of my friends who have been sexually assualted. They felt alone and as if no one understood what they went through. I saw a need for a resource to become available so help people look towards the brighter side of life. I also noticed how high the suicide rates for victims was and felt as if resources were more available to victims, it could help.

Users will be able to acess not only resources but sucess stories and a help line. A lot of user research I have done has asked for a chat room of sorts to talk to other victims so they can truly feel understood, so this will be another feature available. To keep them safe their will be certain words and phrases banned from our site. We understand that this project, is a sensitive one. We aim to help not hinder our users. Thats why around the site we plan to implement warnings where there is potential triggering matieral. We hope that this project will open up more possibilities for our users. Users will gain a community in which everyone will be accepted. They will no longer feel as if its them vs the world but that the world can help them grow. We hope that the user will gain confidence and the support they need to succeed in their life. The user will also gain access to resources they need and this will help them understand that it is okay to ask for help. I have developed a persona to represent what the average user could look like and gain from the website. Persona: Age 20, Female, unknown ethnicity, university student Name: Amelia Amelia was sexually assualted by her best friend, when she told him no he didn't listen and just kept moving in on her. No one could hear her plees for help and these memories keep her from falling asleep at night. Its the next day and she is not sure what to do, her friends won't believe her and police officers scare her. She isn't sure what to do and decides to look it up to see if she can find out what happens when you report a sexually assualt. Alone pops up with a link to the page with the walkthrough of how to report a sexually assualt, what steps to take and most importantly what happens. She decides to report her assualt but it doesn't just stop bothering her. Her nights are hard and many she spends crying in her dorm feeling so alone. Some of her friends don't believe that he could have done it, and make fun of her behind her back. She feels alone, she remembers the website she came across on the first day and relogs on. She discovers forums and stories and genarl support. She no longer feels alone or like everything is her fault. She is able to move forward and see that she is worth it and it is better to keep living then to give up again.
